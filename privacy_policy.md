# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 17, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **Versa** collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot.

## 2. Information We Collect

- **Discord User IDs** — for economy, leveling, and moderation features
- **Discord Server IDs** — for server-specific settings
- **Discord Channel & Role IDs** — for configuration
- **Economy data** — virtual balances
- **XP and level data** — experience points
- **Moderation logs** — warnings, bans, kicks and reasons
- **Ticket history** — ticket status
- **Giveaway entries** — participant IDs
- **Playlist data** — saved songs

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data **solely** to provide Bot features. We do **not**:
- Sell or share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Store regular message content

## 4. Data Storage

- All data is stored in JSON files on the hosting server
- Data is associated with Discord User IDs and Server IDs only
- We do not store usernames, email addresses, or IP addresses

## 5. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as the Bot is active in your server. Users may request deletion of their personal data at any time.

## 6. Third-Party Services

- **YouTube / yt-dlp** — music streaming (no user data shared)
- **Spotify API** — track/playlist resolving (no user data shared)
- **Discord API** — subject to Discord's Privacy Policy

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, delete, or correct your data. Contact us via our support server.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be announced in our support server.
